Vibrdrome

Music Player for Navidrome

A native iOS and macOS music player for your self-hosted Navidrome server. Stream your library, download for offline, and enjoy audiophile features — all from a fast, beautiful interface.

Gapless Playback

Seamless album listening with crossfade and adjustable playback speed.

10-Band Equalizer

Built-in EQ with presets and ReplayGain for consistent volume.

Offline Downloads

Download albums and playlists. Auto-download favorites. Manage cache limits.

Internet Radio

Browse and search thousands of stations. Add custom stream URLs.

Synced Lyrics

Follow along with time-synced lyrics during playback.

Open Source

Free, no ads, no tracking. Your music, your server, your data.
