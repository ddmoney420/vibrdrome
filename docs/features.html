Features

Everything you need to enjoy your music library

Mini Player

  • Capsule-shaped mini player with spinning vinyl album art
  • Circular progress ring shows playback position
  • Tinted background with album art dominant color
  • Swipe left/right for next/previous track
  • Up Next subtitle and quick playback controls
iOS & macOS
Spinning Vinyl

Now Playing

  • Tap song, artist, or album name to navigate to detail pages
  • Heart, 5-star rating, and more menu for quick actions
  • Shuffle and repeat alongside transport controls
  • Custom sliders: thin progress bar with dot, thumbless volume
  • Streaming info with WiFi/download indicators
  • Gapless playback with crossfade support
  • 10-band equalizer with presets and ReplayGain
  • Quick access toolbar: queue, EQ, visualizer, lyrics
iOS & macOS Android Web

Audio Visualizers

  • 18 Metal shader presets on iOS
  • Milkdrop engine on Android & Web
  • Audio-reactive GPU rendering
  • Photosensitivity controls in Settings > Accessibility
iOS & macOS Android Web

Library

  • Customizable layout — reorder & hide sections
  • Genre artwork, decade cards, play history
  • Library folder switching for multi-library servers
  • Smart playlists and artist radio
iOS & macOS Android Web

Album Detail

  • Full-bleed parallax album art that shrinks and fades on scroll
  • Circular frosted glass action buttons (heart, play, shuffle, more)
  • Multi-disc separators and similar albums carousel
  • Artist biography and similar artists on artist pages
iOS

CarPlay & Android Auto

  • Browse your library from your car's dashboard
  • Search, play, and skip from the car
  • Station artwork in CarPlay
iOS Android

Synced Lyrics

  • Tap any line to seek to that position

  • Now Playing widget with album art
  • Siri Shortcuts: "Play my favorites"
  • Toggle playback, skip tracks hands-free

Smart Queue

  • Auto-continues with similar songs when queue runs out
  • Falls back to random songs if no similar found
  • Save current queue as a playlist
  • Queue sharing from the queue menu

Jukebox Mode

  • Play music through your Navidrome server's speakers
  • Full remote control: play, stop, skip, shuffle, volume
  • Queue management with tap-to-skip
  • Add songs from anywhere via context menu
iOS & macOS

Offline Mode

  • Tap the offline banner to browse your downloads instantly
  • Search works offline against downloaded songs
  • Play All / Shuffle your downloaded library
  • Download status indicators on all tracks
  • Offline playback uses local files, no server needed

Web App

  • Full library browsing in the browser
  • Milkdrop visualizer with Butterchurn
  • 8 themes including Retro and Terminal

Scrobbling

  • Sleep timer controls with multiple duration options
  • Digital Crown volume control
