Vibrdrome MilkDrop presets
==========================

The .milk preset files in this directory are ORIGINAL presets authored by the
Vibrdrome project and distributed as part of Vibrdrome:

  vibrdrome_plasma.milk        - original Vibrdrome preset
  vibrdrome_kaleidoscope.milk  - original Vibrdrome preset
  vibrdrome_tunnel.milk        - original Vibrdrome preset

They are NOT third-party community MilkDrop presets. Community presets
(e.g. Geiss, Rovastar, Aderrasi, Zylot, EoS, Mstress, Stahlregen) are
intentionally NOT bundled here, pending license / redistribution clearance.

The projectM engine and MetalANGLE are separately licensed; their attribution
is handled outside this directory (see the project's licensing notes).
